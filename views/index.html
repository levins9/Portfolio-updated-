ABOUT ME

Who am I?

I'm Levin, a passionate graphics designer who specializes in character design.

I've done many past character works of my own as a hobby, and even commissions online.

When I find something mildly interesting, I put my full effort into researching and understanding it whole.

My Approach

My design philosophy centers on creating characters with personality and depth that resonate with audiences.

I believe in iterative design processes, constantly refining my work based on feedback and new insights.

Every project is an opportunity to tell a unique visual story that connects with people emotionally.

Who is this?

Fivecy the Seal

This is my mascot, Fivecy the Seal, who represents my creative spirit and love for marine-inspired characters.

What's Your Passion?

Designing has been a childhood dream and passion for me to pursue since young.

Initially I took up coding/programming when I entered post-secondary education, but I've always been an artist at heart.

That doesn't mean that coding was pointless, however, it taught me to be more disciplined and independent, especially when it comes to project-based work.

Learning to collaborate with teammates has been an invaluable experience that enhances my design process.
